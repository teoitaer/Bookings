Mandatory Activities

Before proceeding with the PA BOOKINGS, please ensure you have completed all the mandatory activities on the CAE/Evionica Portal. These activities are crucial for your progression and include:

  • Completing all required online modules
  • Submitting necessary documentation
  • Passing any preliminary assessments

If you have not completed these activities, please do so before submitting this booking form. Failure to complete these mandatory activities may result in delays or cancellation of your booking.
